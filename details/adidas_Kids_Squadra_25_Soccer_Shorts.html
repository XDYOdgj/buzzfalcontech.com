adidas Kids' Squadra 25 Soccer Shorts

Engineered for the next generation of players, these soccer shorts deliver comfort, style, and sustainability in one sleek package. Designed to meet the needs of active young athletes, the Squadra 25 combines performance features with a modern aesthetic to keep kids motivated and comfortable throughout every game and training session.

Lightweight & Breathable

The lightweight construction ensures kids stay cool even during the most intense play. The fabric is designed to regulate body temperature and move with them, making these shorts ideal for practice, matches, and play sessions of all levels.

Adjustable Fit for Freedom

Equipped with an adjustable drawcord waist, these shorts offer a customizable fit that adapts to the player's needs. Whether they're stretching or sprinting, the flexible waistband ensures maximum mobility and comfort throughout the game.

AEROREADY for Moisture Management

With AEROREADY technology, these shorts keep legs dry and fresh by wicking away sweat. This feature keeps young athletes focused and ready to play, regardless of the weather or activity level.

Eco-Friendly Material Choice

These shorts are made using 100% recycled materials, reflecting adidas' dedication to sustainability and responsible manufacturing. Every pair is a step towards a greener future without sacrificing quality or performance.

The Perfect Choice for Active Kids

Whether they're playing on the field or in the backyard, the Squadra 25 Soccer Shorts are built for all-day wear. They combine durability with a stylish look, making them a great option for boys looking for reliable practice clothing that works as hard as they do.
